An initiative by DBIT


Don Bosco Institute of Technology (DBIT) is rated as one of the best engineering college under Mumbai University and approved by AICTE. It is a Salesian Institute which aims at imparting premium technological education to the youth to equip them with the relevant competencies needed by the industrial sector.The Salesians of Don Bosco are pioneers at imparting world class education to the youth and the disadvantaged worldwide.

Founded by St. John Bosco, an Italian saint-educator of the nineteenth century, the Salesians today have nearly two thousand institutions and a presence spread over 132 countries in 5 contin -ents.Emulating the vision of the founder,the Don Bosco Institutes in India have successfully made studentv discover their 'Purpose in Life' in the technology and vocational skill space.

Why green?


There is a variety of reasons to go green, but most come back to supply and demand. We have a limited amount of resources avail -able and more and more people using them up. If we want our future generations to enjoy the same standard of living we've experienced, we need to take action.

DBCL's greenary


DBCL has a huge green cover over its entire campus. DBCL has come forward to develop a study environment boosts health along with nature. Various projects based on environment are carried out in the campus by the massive support of faculty and participation of students.In today's world very less institutes and organization think of promoting environment to such an extent. DBCL has always given its best efforts to educate people and to share their thoughts for the well-being of society and environment. DBCL is also taking efforts to maintain this projects and also to safeguard environmental richness of the campus. We through this website are feeling proud to briefly show-case our green projects and also encourage our society and other organization to implement these ideas in order to support environment.
